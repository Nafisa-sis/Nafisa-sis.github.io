THE DIRECTOR BOARD

The Director board frames the school policy and approves the annual budget. The Board consists of the Chairman and two Directors.



THE MANAGEMENT COMMITTEE

The Director Board appoints the Management Committee Members. The School Management committee consists of 7 members who have expertise in various fields. The committee advices and recommends policy initiatives and provide support for the school leadership for implementing policy decisions aiming to achieve continuous improvements in all domains.
